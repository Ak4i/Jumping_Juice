About


History

The story of Jumpin' Juice began in Seattle when Leonard Howl,
CEO and Founder, went on vacation to Hawaii, and along the way, tasted
some of their fresh and organic shakes. He was so amazed that he asked the
owners to teach him, and that's how Jumpin' Juices
journey began, now serving over 5,000 people.
